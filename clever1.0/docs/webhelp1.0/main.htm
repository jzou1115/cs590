CLEVER 1.0

This program can be used to carry out cluster expansion of protein energies. The cluster expansion method works by dividing protein energy into interaction fragments, which are similar to mutation energies. The framented interactions are collected to predict energy of new sequences.

By Seungsoo Hahn, Orr Ashenberg, and Gevorg Grigoryan
Correspondence: Amy Keating (keating@mit.edu, Keating Lab)
